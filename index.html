Microblading
Natural hair-stroke brows that last 12–18 months

What is Microblading?

Microblading is a semi-permanent eyebrow technique that uses a fine, handheld blade to create individual hair-like strokes in the skin. Each stroke is filled with pigment, resulting in incredibly realistic, natural-looking brows.

Unlike powder or ombre techniques, microblading mimics the appearance of real hair — making it ideal for those with sparse, thin, or uneven brows who want a natural, effortless look.

The Process

  • 1
    Consultation: We discuss your brow goals, analyze your face shape, and choose the ideal shape and color for you.
  • 2
    Mapping & Design: We precisely map and draw your new brow shape for your approval before any pigment is applied.
  • 3
    Numbing: A topical numbing cream is applied for 20–30 minutes for maximum comfort.
  • 4
    Microblading: Fine hair strokes are created with our precision blade, depositing pigment into the skin.
  • 5
    Aftercare & Touch-Up: You receive full aftercare instructions. A touch-up at 6–8 weeks is included.

Ombré Brows
Soft powder gradient for a polished, made-up look

What are Ombré Brows?

Ombré brows use a digital machine to create a soft, powdery gradient effect — lighter at the front, gradually deeper toward the tail. The result looks like perfectly applied brow powder or pencil, but it's permanent.

This technique is highly versatile and works beautifully on all skin types, including oily skin where microblading may not retain as well. It's longer-lasting and requires less touch-up frequency.

  • 1
    Consultation & Color Match: We select the perfect pigment shade to match or complement your natural brows.
  • 2
    Shape Design: Your ideal brow shape is drawn on for approval, using golden-ratio mapping techniques.
  • 3
    Machine Application: Tiny dots of pigment are implanted using an electric device, building the gradient effect.
  • 4
    Healing & Touch-Up: Brows heal beautifully in 2–3 weeks. Touch-up session included at 6–8 weeks.

Lip Blush
Fuller, defined lips with a natural flush of color

What is Lip Blush?

Lip blush is a permanent makeup technique that deposits subtle, natural pigment into the lips to add color, definition, and the appearance of fullness. The result is a soft, "blush" finish — like your lips but better.

It can correct uneven lip lines, enhance lip shape, add symmetry, and create the illusion of a fuller pout. Color choices range from nude-pink to deeper berry tones to match your style.

  • 1
    Color Consultation: We select a pigment that complements your skin tone and desired outcome.
  • 2
    Lip Mapping: We define and balance your lip shape, adding symmetry and dimension where needed.
  • 3
    Numbing: Thorough numbing for a comfortable experience throughout the session.
  • 4
    Pigment Application: Color is tattooed into the lips with soft, blended technique for a natural gradient.

Permanent Eyeliner
Perfectly defined eyes, every single day

What is Permanent Eyeliner?

Permanent eyeliner is a cosmetic tattoo applied along the lash line to create the appearance of fuller lashes, more defined eyes, and a perfectly consistent liner without daily application.

Options range from a subtle lash-line enhancement (natural look) to a classic fine liner or a bolder, more dramatic line. Upper lash line, lower lash line, or both — customized entirely to you.

  • 1
    Style Selection: Choose between lash-line enhancement, thin liner, medium liner, or bold liner.
  • 2
    Preparation: Eye area is cleansed and a topical numbing agent is applied for comfort.
  • 3
    Application: Pigment is carefully deposited along the lash line with precision and care.
  • 4
    Healing: Minor swelling for 24–48h. Final results visible after 4 weeks.

Combination Brows
Microblading strokes + powder shading = perfection

What are Combination Brows?

Combination brows bring together the natural realism of microblading with the depth and longevity of powder shading. Hair strokes define the front of the brow, while gradient shading adds richness and structure through the body and tail.

The result is a dimensional, textured brow that looks both natural and polished — ideal for people who want more defined brows but still want them to look realistic.

  • 1
    Design Phase: Full brow mapping and style design consultation before any work begins.
  • 2
    Microblading Phase: Hair strokes applied to the brow front for a natural look.
  • 3
    Shading Phase: Powder gradient added to the body and tail using a digital machine.
  • 4
    Review & Aftercare: Final result reviewed, aftercare kit and instructions provided.

Touch-Up Sessions
Keep your permanent makeup looking fresh and vibrant

Why Touch-Ups Matter

Permanent makeup fades over time due to sun exposure, skin type, and natural skin cell renewal. Touch-up sessions restore color intensity, refine the shape, and ensure your results continue to look their best.

We recommend your first touch-up at 6–8 weeks after the initial session (included in your package), followed by annual refreshes to maintain optimal color and definition.

  • 1
    Assessment: We review how your permanent makeup has healed and what areas need attention.
  • 2
    Color Matching: Pigment is matched to your existing work and adjusted if desired.
  • 3
    Refresh Application: Faded areas are filled, shape is refined, and overall results are perfected.
